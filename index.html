About Me

I studied Design as well as Psychology at the University of California Davis, experiencing many emphases of design, but specializing in graphic design. Branding and user interface are my two biggest interests in design. Residing in Southern California, I am currently working as a freelance designer.



I have an art background that goes back to my childhood days. Always in awe of capturing beautiful images, objects or messages in a visual form. Getting lessons from art classes and my dad, I enjoy a variety of mediums such as pencil, charcoal, pen, oil and acrylic paint, watercolor, and mix media for sculptures.



I have an admiration for things handmade as well as by computer, and in both respects I find myself comfortable using the tools at hand to come up with novel ideas and solutions.
